## The 4-Phase Roadmap

### Phase 1 — System Audit and Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

> *"Before we can optimize, we have to audit. We're looking for the bottlenecks in your current routine."*

| Week | Focus | Key Objectives |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Ergonomics & Movement | Audit home office setup; establish baseline daily steps; introduce 5-min desk mobility breaks |
| 2 | Nutritional Debugging | Track current inputs without restriction; identify energy-draining foods and crash triggers; introduce protein targets |
| 3 | Minimum Effective Dose | Begin 3x/week strength training (45 mins max); master foundational movements (squat, hinge, push, pull) |
| 4 | Recovery Optimization | Analyze sleep patterns; establish "digital sunset" routine |

| 6 | Nutritional Refactoring | Refine macronutrient balance; strategies for sprint weeks and on-call rotations |
| 7 | Cardiovascular Health | Integrate LISS cardio (brisk walking) for stamina without burnout |
| 8 | Mid-Program Review | Assess progress against baseline; adjust variables; celebrate early wins |
